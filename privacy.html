Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Lane Lords VR ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our game Lane Lords VR (the "Game").

Information We Collect

Lane Lords VR is a single-player offline game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The Game does not require an account, login, or internet connection to play.

Gameplay Data

All gameplay data, including save files, settings, and progress, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The Game itself does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

However, the platform you use to download the Game (such as Meta Quest Store, Steam, or SideQuest) may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • Steam Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, this applies to all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
