What platforms is Lane Lords VR available on?

Lane Lords VR is available on Meta Quest (Quest 2, Quest 3, Quest Pro) and Steam VR for PC VR headsets.

Do I need an internet connection to play?

No! Lane Lords VR is a single-player offline game. Once downloaded, you can play without an internet connection.

I'm experiencing motion sickness. What can I do?

Lane Lords VR includes comfort options! Go to Settings and try enabling Snap Turn and the Comfort Vignette. You can also reduce turn speed or movement speed to find what works for you.

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at support@lanelordsvr.com with a description of the bug, what you were doing when it happened, and your device/platform. Screenshots or video help a lot!

Will there be multiplayer?

Lane Lords VR is currently single-player focused. We're exploring multiplayer options for future updates based on community feedback!

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by the platform where you purchased the game. Please contact Meta Support or Steam Support directly.
